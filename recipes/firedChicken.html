Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Description

This fired chicken recipe delivers a mouthwatering combination of crispy, golden-brown coating and juicy, tender meat. The process begins with marinating the chicken pieces in a mixture of buttermilk and a blend of spices, allowing the flavors to penetrate deeply. The marinated chicken is then dredged in a seasoned flour mixture, ensuring an even coating that crisps up beautifully when fried. Using a deep skillet or a fryer, the chicken is cooked to perfection, achieving a delightful crunch on the outside while maintaining succulent, flavorful meat inside. Perfect for a family meal or a special gathering, this fired chicken recipe is sure to be a hit, offering a satisfying and delicious experience with every bite

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken (cut into eight pieces)
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • paprika
  • white pepper
  • dried rosemary
  • ground thyme
  • dried oregano
  • dried sage
  • cayenne pepper
  • buttermilk
  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • paprika
  • cayenne pepper
  • garlic powder
  • white pepper
  • onion powder

Steps

  1. Toss the chicken pieces in seasonings, then marinate in buttermilk overnight.
  2. Make the seasoned flour.
  3. Dredge each buttermilk-soaked chicken piece in flour and transfer to a plate.
  4. Fry the chicken until an instant read thermometer reads 165 degrees F.
  5. Drain on a cooling rack over a paper towel-lined baking sheet.
